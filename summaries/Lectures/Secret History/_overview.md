# Secret History — Prof. Jiang Xueqin

> A 28-lecture series exploring the hidden mechanisms of power, evil, and societal collapse throughout human history. Prof. Jiang examines how power actually works, why societies fail, and the recurring patterns of evil from ancient civilizations through the modern era.

---

## About the Lecturer

Prof. Jiang Xueqin brings a historian's perspective to the mechanics of power and societal failure. His Secret History series connects ancient patterns of collapse — gerontocracy, meritocracy failures, bureaucratic decay — to the recurring structures of evil and empire across civilizations.
